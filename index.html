About

Manor Carnie Blast is a thrilling match-3 puzzle game where players enter a magical carnival world to restore an old manor to its former glory. Match colorful carnival-themed items, solve challenging puzzles, and unlock exciting rewards to bring the joy of the carnival back to life.

With a captivating story and whimsical characters, Manor Carnie Blast is perfect for fans of puzzles, design, and carnival fun!

How to Play

  • Uncover
    the Story

    Progress through the game to reveal hidden secrets and expand the carnival world.

  • Participate in Events

    Take part in seasonal challenges and win exclusive decorations for the manor.

  • Use Power-Ups

    Unlock and strategically use boosters like fireworks or popcorn blasts to tackle tough levels.

  • Complete Objectives

    Finish level-specific tasks like collecting items or clearing obstacles.

  • Match
    Items

    Combine three or more carnival-themed objects (e.g., balloons, popcorn) to clear them and earn points.

  • Earn
    Coins

    Use coins earned in levels to renovate and decorate the manor with your unique style.
